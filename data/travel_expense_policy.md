# Corporate Travel & Expense Policy

## 1. Travel Approval
All business travel involving flights or overnight stays must be approved by a Department Head at least 14 days in advance via the "TravelPort" portal.

## 2. Air Travel
Employees must book Economy Class for all flights under 6 hours. Business Class is permitted only for international flights exceeding 6 hours in duration. All bookings must be made through the corporate travel agent.

## 3. Accommodation
Hotel stays are capped at $250 per night in standard cities and $350 per night in high-cost cities (e.g., NY, SF, London, Tokyo). Minibar expenses are personal and will not be reimbursed.

## 4. Meals and Entertainment
The daily meal allowance (per diem) is $75. Receipts are required for any single meal expense over $25. Client entertainment expenses are capped at $150 per person and require prior approval.

## 5. Expense Submission
Expense reports must be submitted within 30 days of trip completion. Late submissions will require CFO approval for reimbursement.
