# Global Remote Work Policy 2025

## 1. Purpose
This policy outlines the guidelines for employees working remotely. The company supports flexible working arrangements to promote work-life balance while ensuring business continuity.

## 2. Eligibility
Full-time employees are eligible for remote work after completing their 90-day probationary period. Contractors and interns are required to work from the office unless specified in their contract.

## 3. Working Hours and Availability
Remote employees must be available during core business hours (10:00 AM to 3:00 PM EST). Employees are expected to respond to internal communications (Slack/Email) within 2 hours during these times.

## 4. Equipment and Security
The company provides a laptop and a headset. Employees must use the company VPN when accessing internal servers. Working from public Wi-Fi networks without VPN is strictly prohibited. 

## 5. Reimbursement
The company does not reimburse costs for home internet or electricity. However, a one-time stipend of $500 is provided for home office ergonomic equipment (chair, desk, or monitor).
